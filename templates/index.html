About Anemia and Indicators

Hemoglobin

Hemoglobin is a protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen. Normal range: 11.6–15 g/dL.

MCH

Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin – average hemoglobin per red cell. Normal range: 19–32 pg.

MCHC

Concentration of hemoglobin in a given volume of packed red blood cells. Normal: 29–36 g/dL.

MCV

Mean Corpuscular Volume – average size of red blood cells. Normal: 72–106 fL.
